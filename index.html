Biography

Currently, I am pursuing my master's degree at China University of Mining & Technology-Beijing under the guidance and supervision of Prof. Guoying Zhang, from whom I have learned a lot. Meanwhile, I am jointly trained at the School of Vehicle and Mobility of Tsinghua University during my master's program and work in the Tsinghua Mengshi Driverless Lab.

My research interests include visual perception in autonomous driving scenarios and Nerf-based generative modeling.
